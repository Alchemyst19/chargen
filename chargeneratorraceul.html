Human

Humans are the most adaptable and ambitious people among the common races. They live fully in the present - making them well-suited to adventuring life - but also plan for the future, striving to leave a lasting legacy. Individually and as a group, humans are adaptable opportunists, and they stay alert to changing social and political dynamics.

Age

Humans reach adulthood in their late teens and live less than a century.

Alignment

Humans tend towards no particular alignment.

Size

Humans vary widely in height and build, from barely 5 feet to well over 6 feet tall. Your size is Medium.

Speed

Your base walking speed is 30 feet.

Languages

You can speak, read and write Common and one extra language of your choice.
