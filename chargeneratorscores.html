Score Descriptions

Strength: Your character's physical prowess and athleticism.

Dexterity: Your character's hand-eye coordination and finesse, tied to acrobatics, evasion, and initiative.

Constitution: Your character's fortitude, affecting your HP and physical saves.

Intelligence: Your character's mental capacity for information and learning, tied to "knowledge" skills and to investigation.

Wisdom: Your character's willpower, affecting your insight and saves against harmful spells.

Charisma: Your character's social aptitude, affecting skills such as Persuasion and Deception.
