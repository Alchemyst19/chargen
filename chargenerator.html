5eCG currently only supports Player's Handbook content. Other races, subclasses, spells and more coming soon!

Race

Your character's species, picked from the list of humanoid ("player races") in D&D5e. Includes everything from humans and elves to kenku and warforged.

Class

Your character's role in combat, from the heavily-armored fighter to the spell-slinging wizard.

Ability Scores

The six numbers which constitute the core of your character: Strength, Dexterity, Consitution, Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma.

Skills

Your character's abilities in select areas, based on your Ability Scores. Includes everything from athleticism to survivalism

Note: Skills go beyond the scope of this site. Please see the Player's Handbook p. 165 for more information on feats.

Spells

The list of spells that your character knows and can cast, chosen from a master spell list.

Note: Spells go beyond the scope of this site. For the master spell lists and more information, see Player's Handbook p. 207 and Xanathar's Guide p. 147.
