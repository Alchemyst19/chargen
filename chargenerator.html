5e Character Generator

Generator Randomizer About

Race

Your character's species, picked from the list of humanoid ("player races") in D&D5e. Includes everything from humans and elves to kenku and warforged.

Class

Your character's role in combat, from the heavily-armored fighter to the spell-slinging wizard.

Subclass

The flavor of your class, typically chosen at Level 3. Every class has a variety of subclasses to encompass a wide range of playstyles.

Feats

Various special perks your character can choose from at levels 4, 8, 12, and 16. Often improve on one particular aspect of your character, such as speed or perception

Ability Scores

The six numbers which constitute the core of your character: Strength, Dexterity, Consitution, Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma.

Skills

Your character's abilities in select areas, based on your Ability Scores. Includes everything from athleticism to survivalism

Spells

The list of spells that your character knows and can cast, chosen from a massive list (including Player's Handbook and Xanathar's Guide to Everything)
