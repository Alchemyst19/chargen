Dwarf

Shaped from stone by Moradin himself, the dwarves' knowledge of stone and metal is unmatched.

Ability Score Increase: Your Constitution increases by 2

Age: Dwarves mature at the same rate as humans, but live for about 350 years.

Alignment: Dwarves tend towards lawful and good alingments.

Size: Dwarves stand between 4 and 5 feet tall and average around 150 pounds. Your size is Medium.

Speed: Your base walking speed is 25 feet. Your speed is not reduced by wearing heavy armor.

Languages: You can speak, write and read Common and Dwarvish.

Darkvision: You can see in dim light within 60 feet of you as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light.

Dwarven Resilience: You have advantage on saving throws against poison, and resistance against poison damage.

Dwarven Combat Training: You have proficiency with the battleaxe, handaxe, throwing hammer, and warhammer

Tool Proficiency: You gain proficiency with either smith's tools, brewer's supplies, or mason's tools.

Stonecunning: Whenever you make a History check related to stonework, you can add double your proficiency bonus to the check, instead of normal proficiency.

Subraces: You can choose between either Hill or Mountain Dwarf

Hill Dwarf

Ability Score Increase: Your Wisdom score increases by 1

Dwarven Toughness: Your hit point maximum increases by 1, and it increases by 1 each time you gain a level.

Mountain Dwarf

Ability Score Increase: Your Strength score increases by 1

Dwarven Armor Training: You have proficieny with light and medium armor

Elf

Ability Score Increase: Your Dexterity score increases by 2

Age: An elf typically claims adulthood at age 100 and can live for 750 years

Alignment: Elves lean towards chaotic and good, although drow are typically evil

Size: Elves range from under 5 to over 6 feet tall, and have slender builds. Your size is Medium

Speed: Your base walking speed is 30 feet

Languages: You can speak, read and write Common and Elvish

Darkvision: You can see in dim light within 60ft as if it were bright light, and darkness as if it were dim light.

Keen Senses: You have proficiency in the Perception skill.

Fey Ancestry: You have advantage on saving throws against being charmed, and magic can't put you to sleep.

Trance: Rather than sleep, elves meditate for 4 hours a day to get the benefits of a long rest.

Subrace: You can choose either the High, Wood, or Dark Elves

High Elves

Ability Score Increase: Your Intelligence score increases by 1.

Elf Weapon Training: You have proficiency with the longsword, shortsword, shortbow, and longbow.

Cantrip: You know one cantrip of your choice from the wizard spell list. Intelligecne is your spellcasting ability for it.

Extra Language: You can speak, read and write one extra language of your choice.

Wood Elf

Ability Score Increase: Your Wisdom score increases by 1.

Elf Weapon Training: You have proficiency with the longsword, shortsword, shortbow, and longbow.

Fleet of Foot: Your base walking speed increases to 35 feet.

Mask of the Wild: You can attempt to hide even when you are only lightly obscured by foliage, heavy rain, falling snow, mist, and other natural phenomena.

Dark Elf

Ability Score Increase: Your Charisma score increases by 1.

Superior Darkvision: Your darkvision has a radius of 120 feet.

Sunlight Sensitivity: You have disadvantage on attack rolls and Perception chekcs that rely on sight when you, the target of your attack, or whatever you are trying to perceive is in direct sunlight.

Drow Magic: You know the dancing lights cantrip. When you reach 3rd level, you can cast faerie fire once per day. When you reach 5th level, you can cast darkness once per day. Charisma is your spellcasting ability for these spells.

Drow Weapon Training: You have proficiency with rapiers, shortswords, and hand crossbows.

Halfling

Short and sweet, halflings utilize thier stature to charm others, typically for their own benefit.

Ability Score Increase: Your Dexterity score increases by 2.

Age: Halflings reach adulthood at age 20 and generally live more than two hundred years.

Alignment: Halflings tend towards good alignments, especially lawful good.

Size: Halflings average around 3 feet tall and weigh about 40 pounds. Your size is Small.

Speed: Your base walking speed is 25 feet.

Languages: You can speak, read and write Common and Halfling.

Lucky: When you roll a 1 on an attack roll, ability check, or saving throw, you can reroll the die and must use the new roll.

Lightfoot Halfling

Ability Score Increase: Your Charisma score increases by 1.

Naturally Stealthy: You can attempt to hide even when you are obscured only by a creature that is at least one size larger than yours.

Stout Halfling

Stout Resilience: You have advantage on saving throws against poison, and you have resistance to poison damage.

Human

Humans are the most adaptable and ambitious people among the common races. They live fully in the present - making them well-suited to adventuring life - but also plan for the future, striving to leave a lasting legacy. Individually and as a group, humans are adaptable opportunists, and they stay alert to changing social and political dynamics.

Ability Score Increase: Your ability scores each increase by 1.

Age: Humans reach adulthood in their late teens and live less than a century.

Alignment: Humans tend towards no particular alignment.

Size: Humans vary in height from barely 5 feet to well over 6 feet tall. Your size is Medium.

Speed: Your base walking speed is 30 feet.

Languages: You can speak, read and write Common and one extra language of your choice.

Variant Human

You may choose to take this variant instead, trading your Ability Score Increase from the base human with the following.

Ability Score Increase: Two different ability scores of your choice each increase by 1.

Skills: You gain proficiency in one skill of your choice.

Feat: You gain one feat of your choice.

Dragonborn

Scaled and proud, dragonborn call upon their draconic ancestry to unleash elemental powers upon thier foes.

Ability Score Increase: Your Strength score increases by 2, and your Charisma score increases by 1.

Age: Dragonborn reach adulthood by 15 and live to be around 80.

Alignment: Dragonborn lean towards either good or evil.

Size: Dragonborn are well over 6 feet tall and weigh almost 250 pounds. Your size is Medium.

Speed: Your base walking speed is 30 feet.

Languages: You can speak, read and write Common and Draconic.

Draconic Ancestry: Choose one type of dragon. Your breath weapon and damage resistance are determined by the dragon type, as shown in the table in the Player's Handbook.

Breath Weapon: You can use your action to exhale destructive energy, as determined by your Draconic Ancestry. Creatures in the area must make a saving throw, the DC for which is (8 + your CON + your proficiency bonus). Creatures take 2d6 damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one. The damage increases as you gain levels.

Damage Resistance: You have resistance to the damage type associated with your Draconic Ancestry.

Age: Gnomes typically settle down around age 40 and live between 350 and 500 years.

Size: Gnomes are between 3 and 4 feet tall and average 40 pounds. Your size is Small.

Forest Gnome

Ability Score Increase: Your Dexterity score increases by 1.

Natural Illusionist: You know the minor illusion cantrip. Intelligence is your spellcasting ability for it.

Speak with Small Beasts: You can communicate simple ideas to Small or smaller beasts.

Rock Gnome

Ability Score Increase: Your Constitution score increases by 1.

Artificer's Lore: You add twice your proficiency bonus to History checks related to magic items, alchemical objects, or technological devices.

Tinker: You have proficiency with tinker's tools. Using those tools, you can spend 1 hour and 10gp of materials to construct a Tiny clockwork device, from the options listed in the Player's Handbook. The object stops functioning after 24 hours unless you spend 1 hour repairing it, or when you use your action to dismantle it; at that time, you can retain the materials used to create it. You can have up to 3 devices active at a time.
